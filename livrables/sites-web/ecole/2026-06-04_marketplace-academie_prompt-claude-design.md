---

## PROMPT

Design a premium marketplace website called **"Apreneur Market"** — a platform that connects AI experts and graduates from a French business academy with companies looking to hire them for AI implementation, digital transformation, and specialized training missions.

---

### Brand & Tone

- **Name:** Apreneur Market
- **Tagline:** *"L'expertise IA, à portée de mission."*
- **Audience:** Two sides — (1) Expert freelancers & recent graduates in AI, and (2) SMBs and enterprises looking to hire AI specialists
- **Tone:** Modern, trustworthy, premium but accessible. Think Linear meets Malt — clean, confident, conversion-focused.
- **Language:** French (UI labels and copy in French)

---

### Visual Identity

- **Color palette:**
  - Primary: Deep indigo `#1E1B4B` (trust, expertise)
  - Accent: Electric violet `#7C3AED` (innovation, AI)
  - Highlight: Warm amber `#F59E0B` (energy, opportunity)
  - Background: Off-white `#FAFAFA` with soft gray `#F3F4F6` cards
  - Text: Near-black `#111827`
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif — Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans. Bold headlines, generous whitespace.
- **Style:** Glassmorphism cards, subtle gradients, micro-interactions suggested. No heavy illustrations — prefer clean icons and real photography placeholders.

---

### Pages to Design

#### 1. Homepage (main priority)

**Hero section:**
- Large headline: *"Trouvez l'expert IA qu'il vous faut. En 48h."*
- Sub-headline: *"Apreneur Market connecte les entreprises avec des experts et diplômés certifiés en intelligence artificielle, prêts pour vos missions."*
- Two CTAs side by side:
  - Primary button (violet): "Je cherche un expert"
  - Secondary button (outline): "Je propose mes services"
- Background: Abstract dark gradient with subtle AI-inspired particle or neural network visual

**Trust bar:**
- Row of logos: "Ils nous font confiance" — 5-6 placeholder company logos
- Stats: "300+ experts certifiés · 120+ entreprises · 95% de missions réussies"

**How it works (3 steps):**
- Step 1: "Publiez votre besoin" — icon: document
- Step 2: "Recevez des profils qualifiés" — icon: users
- Step 3: "Lancez votre mission" — icon: rocket
- Clean numbered cards, horizontal layout

**Expert categories (service areas):**
Grid of 6 clickable cards with icon + label:
1. Implémentation IA (automatisation, LLM, agents)
2. Formation & montée en compétences
3. Stratégie & transformation digitale
4. Data & Analytics
5. Intégration no-code / low-code
6. Secteur Santé & IA
Each card has a hover effect with violet accent.

**Featured expert profiles (3 cards):**
Each card shows: Avatar placeholder, Name, Specialty tag, Star rating, Hourly rate range, "Voir le profil" button
Example profiles:
- "Aminata D. — Experte IA & Automatisation — ⭐ 4.9 — 120-180€/h"
- "Lucas M. — Formateur IA en entreprise — ⭐ 4.8 — 90-140€/h"
- "Sarah K. — Data Scientist & LLM — ⭐ 5.0 — 150-200€/h"

**For companies section:**
Two-column layout:
- Left: Bold copy — "Externalisez votre IA. Sans risque." + 4 bullet benefits + CTA "Déposer une mission"
- Right: A clean mockup of a mission posting form (visual only)

**For experts section:**
Dark indigo background, two-column:
- Left: A mockup profile card
- Right: "Valorisez votre expertise. Travaillez avec les meilleurs." + 3 benefits + CTA "Rejoindre la communauté"

**Testimonials:**
3 quote cards alternating between company clients and expert freelancers

**FAQ accordion:**
5 questions covering: how vetting works, payment security, mission types, average timelines, academy certification badge

**Footer:**
Logo + tagline, nav links (À propos, Comment ça marche, Experts, Entreprises, Blog, Contact), social icons, legal links

---

#### 2. Expert Profile Page

Clean two-column layout:
- Left sidebar: Photo avatar, name, specialty, location (remote), availability badge (green dot "Disponible"), rating, number of missions, hourly rate, "Contacter" CTA button, skills tags
- Right main area: About section, Services offered (3 cards with price), Past missions (anonymized), Certifications (Apreneur Académie badge prominent), Reviews

---

#### 3. Mission Board (listing page)

- Search bar + filters (category, budget range, duration, remote/on-site)
- Grid of mission cards, each showing: Company logo placeholder, mission title, category tag, budget, duration, date posted, "Postuler" button
- Sidebar with active filters

---

### Key UI Components to Include

- **Expert card** with hover lift effect
- **Mission card** with status badge (Ouvert / En cours / Pourvu)
- **Search bar** with category dropdown
- **Certification badge** — "Certifié Apreneur Académie" with a small logo seal
- **Availability toggle** on expert profiles
- **Progress stepper** for mission posting flow
- **Notification bell** in nav

---

### Navigation Bar

Logo left — Links center: "Trouver un expert | Déposer une mission | Comment ça marche | Blog" — Right: "Se connecter | S'inscrire" (violet button)

Sticky on scroll, slight blur background on scroll.

---

Design should be responsive-first. Show mobile version of the hero and expert card components.
